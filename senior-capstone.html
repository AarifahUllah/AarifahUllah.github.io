My year-long senior capstone design project was participating in Raytheon's Autonomous Vehicle Competition, a nation-wide inter-collegiate competition. We were tasked to design, build, and program unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) to complete a series of challenges.

I was honored to serve as the Team Captain for our 13-person interdisciplinary team across Computer Science, Mechanical and Engineering disciplines. Some of my responsibilities included leading team meetings, communicating with our sponsors, Raytheon, and stakeholders. I also managed the budget and contributed significantly to the algorithmic development, autonomous mission programming, and real-world drone deployment.

Our school won the Most Innovative Design Award for performing the best at competition day by having the best times. We also had a creative approach to the challenges that was atypical and noticed by the judges.

I am also getting a patent as the sole inventor for designing an efficient method for autonomous navigation.
