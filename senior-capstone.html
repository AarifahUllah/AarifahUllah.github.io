My year-long senior capstone design project was participating in Raytheon's Autonomous Vehicle Competition. It is a nation-wide inter-collegiate competition where we were tasked to design, build, and program unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) to complete a series of challenges.

I was honored to serve as the Team Captain for our 13-person interdisciplinary team across Computer Science, Mechanical and Engineering disciplines. Some of my responsibilities included leading team meetings, communicating with our sponsors, Raytheon, and stakeholders.
