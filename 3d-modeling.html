Currently, I'm working as a Research Assistant at the Intelligent Autonomous Systems Lab (IASL) under Prof. Peng Wei. Funded by NASA, FAA, and NSF, I look into drone delivery in densely populated and suburban areas.

I'm looking into a 3D-visualization feature that lets the user see what the drone perceives using Unity. This is useful in object avoidance and collision, especially since drones share the same space as other drones but also birds and wildlife.

In addition to that, I work with IsaacSim in in developing the drone behavior in a simulated world, and deploy it to a real-life chassis. The drone utilizes multiple camera sensors and a vision language model. Much emphasis is given towards safety with different protocols in place for when persons are present.
